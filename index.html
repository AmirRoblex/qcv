About Me

I'm Seyed Amir Muhammad (or SAM for short), a Geomatics engineer and data analyst greeting from Rasht, Iran.
My academic journey led me to excel at the University of Zanjan, where I graduated as the valedictorian of my class. I now embark on captivating research in spatial data science, channeling my passion into innovative geospatial applications. With proficiency spanning Python, R, JavaScript, and MATLAB, alongside a toolkit encompassing GIS, statistics, and web development, I relish delving into diverse data science realms to engineer practical solutions.
Driven by a fervent enthusiasm for geomatics and data science, I am actively seeking opportunities for advanced academic pursuits, whether through a master's or Ph.D. program, to propel my research journey and contribute to transformative advancements in the field.
